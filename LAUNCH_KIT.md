## App Store Description (4000 chars max)

```
Pixel Terrarium is a tiny living world in your pocket.

Build an ecosystem out of pixels. Plant a forest, watch ants discover it, then drop a queen and let the colony establish. Spiders move in along the canopy. Wasps follow the spiders. Termites burrow into old wood, fungi decompose the fallen, and the whole web hums along on its own.

Or skip the harmony entirely. Drop a Replicator virus in the middle of a thriving colony and watch every plant get rewritten cell by cell. Light a fire and let Pyrocast carry it through the forest. Pour acid from a programmable cloud. Crank the entropy slider and let the world devour itself.

WHAT'S IN THE BOX

• A real cellular simulation — 24,000 cells, eight species with full life cycles, genome-based speciation, and emergent behavior. No scripted events; everything that happens emerges from the rules.

• Eight cellular automaton viruses including Conway's Game of Life, HighLife, Replicator, Bloom, Inversion, Phantom (Brian's Brain), Pyrocast (forest fire), and Maze. Each one interacts with the living ecosystem in its own way.

• Element Lab — design your own elements with traits like falls, flows, burns, glows, and decays. They get a permanent slot in your toolbox.

• Creature Lab — design your own creatures with diet, behavior, lifespan, and reproduction rules.

• Programmable clouds and voids — point them at whatever you want, place them on the map, let the world become a chemistry set.

• Pause, scrub speed, sun position, brush size, mutation rate, entropy filter, low-power mode — every knob you'd want, none of the ones you don't.

NO ACCOUNTS. NO NETWORK. NO ADS. NO IAP. NO TRACKING.

Everything in Pixel Terrarium runs entirely on your device. Your saved elements, your creatures, your worlds — none of it ever leaves your phone. We collect nothing.

For ages 4+. Best with headphones on a quiet afternoon.
```

(Character count: ~1,500. App Store allows up to 4,000.)

## App Privacy questionnaire (App Store Connect)

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Does this app collect data? | **No** |
| Does the app use third-party SDKs that collect data? | **No** |
| Does the app use tracking? | **No** |

You'll get a "Data Not Collected" privacy nutrition label — clean and simple.

## What's blocked until you ship

This is the only audit-flagged blocker remaining: **App Store Connect record + Apple Developer account setup.** I cannot do this part — you have to:

1. Log into <https://appstoreconnect.apple.com> with your Apple Developer account ($99/yr)
2. Click "My Apps" → "+" → "New App"
3. Fill in the basics from this Launch Kit
4. Once the record exists, Xcode's "Archive" → "Distribute App" can upload to it
